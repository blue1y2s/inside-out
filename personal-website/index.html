About SeeYourself

What if you could visualize your inner personality as a universe of glowing spheres, each reflecting a unique facet of who you are?

SeeYourself brings your emotional landscape to life. By analyzing text and personality dimensions, it creates a 3D universe of "memory spheres" where colors act as a visual language for deep self-understanding.
